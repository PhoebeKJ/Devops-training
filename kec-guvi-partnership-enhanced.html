Partnership Journey

Milestones in our collaborative effort to revolutionize tech education

1984

KEC Foundation

Kongu Engineering College established by Kongu Vellalar Institute of Technology Trust, beginning a legacy of excellence in engineering education.

2014

GUVI Launch

GUVI founded and incubated by IIT Madras and IIM Ahmedabad, pioneering vernacular tech education in India.

2022

HCL Acquisition

GUVI acquired by HCL Group, expanding reach and capabilities with enterprise backing.

2024

Partnership Begins
